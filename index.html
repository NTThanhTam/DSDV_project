World instant noodle consumption

Welcome to the fascinating world of noodle consumption! Noodles have become a staple food in many cultures around the world, representing not only a delicious and comforting meal but also a significant part of culinary traditions. In this project, we delve into the global landscape of noodle consumption, exploring trends, regional variations, and the cultural significance of this beloved dish.
